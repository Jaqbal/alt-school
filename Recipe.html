Eba and Egusi Soup

Eba served with Egusi soup is a staple in many Nigerian homes. It is filling, flavorful, and perfect for any occasion.

Recipe Information

Prep time: 20 minutes

Cook time: 50 minutes

Servings: 5 people

Difficulty: Intermediate

Ingredients

  • 2 cups ground egusi
  • 2 cooking spoons palm oil
  • 500g assorted meat
  • 200g stockfish and dried fish
  • 2 tablespoons ground crayfish
  • 2 seasoning cubes
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 1 tablespoons ground pepper
  • 2 cups spinach
  • Salt to taste
  • 4 cups water
  • 2 cups garri (for eba)

Instructions

  1. Wash and season meat with onion, salt, and one seasoning cube. Boil until tender.
  2. Add stockfish and dried fish to the pot and cook for 10 minutes.
  3. Heat palm oil in a separate pot, add chopped onion, and fry lightly.
  4. Stir in ground egusi and fry until it thickens into lumps.
  5. Add meat stock gradually while stirring. Simmer for 20 minutes.
  6. Mix in ground crayfish, pepper, remaining seasoning cube, and salt. Simmer for 10 minutes.
  7. Add washed spinach or ugu leaves and cook for 5 minutes.
  8. For eba, boil 4 cups of water. Sprinkle in garri while stirring until smooth and firm.
  9. Serve hot egusi soup with eba.

Tips

Use fresh vegetables for the best taste. Do not overcook the leaves to preserve nutrients.
